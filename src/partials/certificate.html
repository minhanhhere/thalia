What I’m Proud to Be Certified In

May 2021

FSPCA Preventive Controls for Human Food (2021)​

A certification program developed by the Food Safety Preventive Controls Alliance (FSPCA), covering FDA requirements for hazard analysis and risk-based preventive controls under the FSMA. It qualifies participants to become Preventive Controls Qualified Individuals (PCQI) for food facilities.

Feb 2022

HACCP for Dry Food Products (2022)

A certification focused on applying Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) principles to the production and handling of dry food products. It ensures compliance with food safety standards by identifying and controlling potential hazards throughout the manufacturing process.​

Mar 2025

Scrum Fundamentals Certified (SFC), 2025​

An entry-level certification that covers the basics of Scrum principles, roles, events, and artifacts. It provides a solid foundation for individuals new to Agile and Scrum methodologies.​
